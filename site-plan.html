Overview

Purpose

Have you ever seen yourself inspiring others? Especially doing what you love? That is what I do, I provide counseling to people who love inspiring others on what they love doing and are good at. Having professional teaching skills is crucial to help everyone to understand, learn and apply. My platform prepares talented people and at the same time enables them to reach many of those who are interested in what they do. Many have become part of it and inspired thousands on my platform.

Audience

Welcome to Inspire! This website is prepared specially for adolescents and young adults that want to help and influence others by doing what they love. Here you will learn what to do to become successful and also how to apply the knowledge that we will provide you. Technology is a requirement to extend horizons. We look forward to helping you to be recognized in your most talented area and have the opportunity to find people that like what you do. Are you ready for it?

Home page

For Inspires: If you have something to share that you love with others and want to inspire others to act, if you want to be recognized for what you are good at and generate passion in them, this is the place. On this website, you will learn how to develop teaching strategies that will facilitate your path to your goal and vision. You will also have the great opportunity to meet new people and create communities through technology. You are not good at technology? No needs to worry. We will teach you how to use it simply so that you may extend your horizons and have success in this new journey.

Who We Are

We are Inspire and we help talented people to become recognize in what they do while also motivating others. Our goal is to help you to be successful and inspire people by doing what you love. How? We show and teach you what things you need in order to influence and reach thousands through technology. We are truly committed into helping you. This is why we help you providing all of the necessary tools along your journey on the Inspire platform.

First, we need you to have the following items with you:

  • Cellphone
  • Paper
  • Pen
  • What you use (For example, if I play the guitar, I would have my guitar with me)

Second, copy the following questions:

  • What do you want to show?
  • What are you going to say?
  • How long is it going to last?(It should be less than a minute)
  • What kind of edition can I add to my video?

Finally, answer them.

Inspire

Inspirators prefer this website because it is accessible and completely free. It has a good pace, the presentation is very simple and anyone can apply what is taught there. It provides the best and easiest ways to inspire others increasing the number of people watching and also creating good quality content. It also helps you to achieve one single thing that is to inspire others doing what they love to do. Everything in this website is centered to help you to achieve your goal and vision.

A requirement for you to apply what you are taught; you need to have a TikTok account and internet access. We believe TikTok is the best option for those who want to grow fast in the audience. Tik Tok has the best algorithms to find people that may be interested in what you do. It also provides you the option to edit your video with music and effects that may improve your audience experience. Finally, you will know who liked the video or started to follow you. This may be a great opportunity to contact them and invite them to be part of your community.
